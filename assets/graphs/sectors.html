A sector is a group of companies or industries that share similar business activities. Sectors help organize and analyze the economy.

Technology

Companies like AAPL, MSFT, and IBM belong to the technology sector. They focus on developing and selling tech products and services.

Financial Services

Banks like JPM, BAC, and C are part of the finance sector. They manage money and financial assets.

Healthcare

Companies like JNJ, LLY, and MRK make up the healthcare sector. They provide health services and products.

Energy

Oil, gas, and renewable energy companies are in the energy sector. They produce and distribute energy resources.

Industrials

Companies like BA, CAT, and GE belong to Industrials. They produce machinery, aircraft, and construction supplies.

Consumer Cyclical

Companies like HD, MCD, and NKE belong to Consumer Cyclical. These rely on consumer spending (retail, automotive, etc.).
