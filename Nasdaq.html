What is NASDAQ?

NASDAQ is an American stock exchange located in New York City. It is the second-largest exchange in the world by market capitalization, after the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE).

History of NASDAQ

Founded in 1971, NASDAQ was the world's first electronic stock market. It has a rich history of technological innovation in the financial industry.
